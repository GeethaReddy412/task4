Swami Vivekananda Degree and P.G. College Swami Vivekananda Degree College, top degree college in Mahabubnagar is established in the year 2002 at Mahabubnagar with several courses in science and commerce at undergraduate level. This college is affiliated to Osmania University. SVDC is a self financed institution structured on a solid foundation of sound academic policies and principles. The college is one of the top degree colleges in Telangana which is co-educational and imparts education in both Telugu and English medium. Students are exposed to experienced teaching that is merged with “state of the art” observational facilities. Having impressive faculty members and senior academicians on advisory board SVDC is ever alert to the standards of education imparted and maintains strict vigilance on the Quality of instruction.
